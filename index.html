About me

I'm Computer Engineer Student at Universidad de Zaragoza, Spain. Currently I'm finishing my master's degree in Computer Engineering (at the same University) while working as a researcher at I3A. My areas of main interest are IoT, Linux Kernel, power, thermal, control and performance.
