About me

Performance modelling engineer in Arm, helping to design the new generation of Arm Cores.

I studied Computer Engineering at the University of Zaragoza, Spain. And a master's degree while working as a researcher at I3A. I am most interested in the areas of IoT, Linux Kernel, power, thermal, control and performance. My bachelor's dissertation was focused on the control of temperature in mobile processors by using formal control techniques, a thesis that is being extended for my master's dissertation. I presented my work at the ARM Research Summit 2018 in Cambridge, link to the video and Jornadas Sarteco 2018 in Teruel, Spain, link to the paper (in Spanish)DOI 10.5281/zenodo.1403431.
